Welcome to Gctechie

ESports turns online gaming into a spectator sport.
It mimics the experience of watching a professional
sporting event, except instead of watching a physical
event, spectators watch video gamers compete against
each other. The eSports trend has become so widespread
in recent years that games can often be viewed at an
organized arena event.As with traditional sports,
these games are broken down into competitive leagues and tournaments.
